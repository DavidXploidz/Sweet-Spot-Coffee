About us

An unmissable stopover,
finger-licking good

More than just a coffee shop, we started Sweet Spot Coffee with a simple idea: to create a cozy space where great coffee and good company come together. We believe that every cup tells a story, and we are passionate about sharing that story with our community.

Our mission is to serve great coffee, but also to serve people. We carefully source our beans from Vancouver Canada and our pastries are baked fresh every morning, ensuring every single item on our menu is crafted with care.

This is a place where you can find your perfect moment—whether you're catching up with friends, getting work done, or simply enjoying a quiet moment to yourself. We invite you to step inside, find your favorite spot, and savor the experience.
